Sirlion Steak

Prep Time Cook Time Additional Time
7 mins 8 mins 1 hrs
Total Time Servings
1 hrs 15 2

Ingredients

  • 1 pound steak
  • 2 ½ tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
  • ¼ teaspoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions

Step 1 : Mix the steak with black pepper, rosemary,garlic, parsley,and olive oil.Leave it in the fridge for an hour.

Step 2 : Heat a pan and add the steak in it. Cook it each side for about 4 minutes. Allow the meal to seat for 5 minutes before Serving
